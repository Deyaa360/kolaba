About Kolaba

Kolaba connects skincare brands with a vetted pool of Turkish micro-creators to produce high-performing UGC: short videos and photos crafted for ads, product pages, and social.

We focus on speed, creative quality, and performance. You brief us once—we handle casting, direction, and delivery.

What we deliver

  • Hook-driven 15–45s videos (ad-ready, vertical).
  • Short product explainers and before/after style demos.
  • 3–8 high-quality photos per video set for PDP and socials.
  • Captions, on-screen text, and export for Meta/TikTok.

What we’re best at

  • Acne, oil control, pores, texture, sensitivity.
  • Serums (Niacinamide, Salicylic, Vitamin C), cleansers, SPF.
  • Routine storytelling (AM/PM), ingredient education, claims clarity.

How it works

  1. 1) Brief — share goals, product, and platforms
    • Tell us your KPI (CTR, CPA, PDP dwell, etc.) and audience.
    • Pick angles: routine, demo, testimonial, ingredient-led.
    • Ship product to creator or send assets if virtual demo is fine.
  2. 2) Match & script — we shortlist creators and write hooks
    • Shortlist matched by skin type/tone, vibe, camera presence.
    • 3–6 opening hooks + CTA frames aligned to your claim-safe copy.
    • You approve creator + script in one pass.
  3. 3) Shoot — creator records per brief
    • Natural light, clean audio, readable on-screen text sizes.
    • Framing for 9:16 by default; 1:1/16:9 on request.
    • Raw takes kept for alternates and future edits.
  4. 4) Edit — cuts, captions, compliance
    • Beat-matched cuts, branded CTA card, safe language review.
    • Readable captions/subtitles; color & audio leveling.
    • Export presets for Meta/TikTok; filenames you can track.
  5. 5) Deliver & iterate — test, learn, repeat
    • 1–2 revision rounds within 5 business days.
    • We suggest next hooks based on your early performance.
    • Usage: ads + web + organic for 3 months (extendable).
